# Privacy Policy

**App name:** EDO Study: Học tốt
**Last updated:** June 15, 2026
**Contact:** eduino.info@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy describes how the Happ application ("the App", "we", "us")
collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the App. By
using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account information (via Google Sign-In)

The App uses **Google Sign-In** and **Firebase Authentication** to let you log
in. When you sign in with your Google account, we request access to the
following Google profile scopes:

- **Email address** (`email` scope)
- **Basic profile information** (`profile` scope), which may include your name
  and profile picture

We do **not** receive or store your Google account password. Authentication is
handled securely by Google and Firebase.

### 1.2 Authentication tokens

After a successful login, the App receives and stores the following on your
device:

- An **access token** and a **refresh token** used to keep you signed in
- A **user identifier (user ID)**
- Basic **user profile data** returned by our service

These items are stored securely on your device (see Section 3).

### 1.3 Academic data you use in the App

To provide its features, the App processes data related to your studies, such as:

- **Subjects** and subject-related information you add or select
- **Academic scores / grades**
- **Countdowns** (e.g., to exams or important dates)
- **Academic timeline / school year** information

This data is sent to and retrieved from our backend service so it can be saved
to your account and shown across sessions.

### 1.4 Information we do NOT collect

- We do not collect your contacts, photos, precise location, microphone, or
  camera data.
- We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

- Authenticate you and keep you signed in to your account
- Provide and personalize the App's core features (subjects, scores, countdowns,
  academic timeline)
- Save and synchronize your data with your account
- Maintain the security and integrity of the service
- Diagnose and fix technical problems

We do **not** use your information for advertising.

---

## 3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

- **On-device sensitive data** (access token, refresh token, user ID, and user
  profile data) is stored using secure platform storage:
  the **iOS Keychain** and the **Android Keystore** (via secure storage).
- Authentication is handled by **Google** and **Firebase Authentication**.
- Data transmitted between the App and our backend is sent over the network to
  our server. We recommend and use secure connections for production traffic.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of
transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

- Your account-related data is retained while your account is active.
- When you **log out**, the App clears all sensitive data stored on your device

- To request **deletion of your account and associated data** from our service,
  contact us at **eduino.info@gmail.com**. We will process your request within a
  reasonable period in accordance with applicable law.
